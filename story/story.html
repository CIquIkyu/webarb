1st SEASON STORY

H age 3rd year
A new entertainment rap batlle「Alternative Rap Battle」, sponsored by Chuohku, was decided to be held.
And many MCs from each division participate in the event.

The prize money is「50 million yen」
Different from the territory battles, the semi-finals and finals will be enlivened by a famous DJ invited by Chuohku

On the day of the semi-finals and finals,
Among the audience who has gathered to see the battle of the winning division teams on Chuohku's battle stage were a DJ and his apprentice.

And the apprentice of Rokuro who trains everyday to become the best DJ to surpass Rokuro is the main protagonist (you).
The main protagonist meets「Buster Bros!!!」,「MAD TRIGGER CREW」,「Fling Posse」,「Matenrou」who are participating in the Alternative Rap Battle.
During his DJ trainings, the protagonist becomes involved in a major incident that goes beyond the scope of an entertainbent battle.

2nd SEASON STORY

Time has passed since the Alternative Rap Battle hosted by Chuohku and despite the various incidents that have occured in the city, the peaceful days have returned.

At this time, rumours spread on the internet, mainly among the young people.
[Charm], an mysterious ornament that is said to occur great events in you life
Getting a high schore in tests, meeting your destined partner, becoming rich.....
The people who flocked to find [Charm] in search of happiness gradually became radical

On the other hand, Doudantsutsuji Rokuro (DJ Rokuro) has gone missing after the Alternative Rap Battle -Revenge-.

The protagonist finds out that the rumored [Charm] is a clue to his mentor, Rokuro's disappearance and begins his search for [Charm] to find Rokuro.

With the help of the 18 people who have returned to their respective diviions, Ikebukuro, Yokohama, Nagoya, Shinjuku, Oosaka and Nagoya,
the protagonist who looks for his mentor has yet to realize that a new threat is approaching....!
