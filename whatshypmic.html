WHAT'S HYPNOSIS MIC

"Hypnosismic" is an original music project that began at September, 2017.
A total of 18 unique characters are divided into six teams of「Ikebukuro Division」, 「Yokohama Division」, 「Shibuya Division」, 「Shinkuju Division」, 「Oosaka Division」, 「Nagoya Division」and they engage in heated rap battles.

Rappers and Trackmakers leading Japan's HIP-HOP scene take part in the creation of the rap music to color the battles.
The voice actors would act while rapping various songs and each character's story is developed around the music.

"Hypnosismic" is also a multi-media project including comics, a game application, a stage and an anime based strong charactered related songs and voice drama focused on stories.
